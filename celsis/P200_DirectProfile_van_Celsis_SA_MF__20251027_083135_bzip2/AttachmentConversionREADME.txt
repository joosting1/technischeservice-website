
In the future we will be storing and providing product attachments in a different way.

However this also means that download urls such as https://att.2ba.nl/<GLN>/master/PPI/<filename> will become https://att.2ba.nl/<GLN>/PPI/c0/c0c8/c0c8542e86907655140d81c9699e7a4c.jpg.

Only the url will change without the content of the file changing.

For now both new and old urls will work, in the future only new urls will work and be exported.

We have included a comma-seperated value (.csv) file that includes the current attachment urls as well as the future attachment urls.

Please see AttachmentConversion.csv

Column 1: GLN can be used to identify the relevant product
Column 2: ProductCode can be used to identify the relevant product
Column 3: FileHash is used in our internal systems to organise files and may or may not be relevant to you
Column 4: AttachmentLocation is the attachment url that we are currently exporting
Column 5: AttachmentLocationNew is the attachment url that we will be exporting after the switchover sometime later this year, this will be either your own location or an att.2ba link that uses the FileHash to identify files
Column 6: CreateDate refers to when this attachment was created/uploaded to us
Column 7: MutationDate refers to when this attachment was last changed in any way

For any further questions or inquiries please contact helpdesk@2ba.nl with the subject "Attachment Conversion"

In de toekomst gaan wij product attachments op een andere manier opslaan en laten downloaden.

Maar dit betekend ook dat download urls zoals https://att.2ba.nl/<GLN>/master/PPI/<filename> gaan veranderen naar https://att.2ba.nl/<GLN>/PPI/c0/c0c8/c0c8542e86907655140d81c9699e7a4c.jpg.

Alleen de url veranderd, maar de inhoud van de file blijft hetzelfde.

Voor nu werken zowel nieuwe als oude urls, in de toekomst zullen alleen nieuwe urls werken en worden geexporteerd.

We hebben een comma-seperated value (.csv) file toegevoegd die de huidige attachment urls en de toekomstige attachment urls bevat.

Zie hiervoor AttachmentConversion.csv

Heeft u nog vragen of opmerkingen neem contact via helpdesk@2ba.nl met vermelding "Attachment Conversion"
